# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Conceptual Tasks

## Overview

This document defines conceptual tasks aligned with each module of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. These tasks are designed to reinforce theoretical understanding, promote critical thinking, and facilitate the application of concepts learned in each module. The tasks emphasize academic rigor and learning objectives rather than implementation details, focusing on comprehension, analysis, and synthesis of knowledge.

The conceptual tasks are structured to support the pedagogical progression established in the academic plan, with each task building upon previous knowledge while introducing new challenges and learning opportunities. Tasks are designed to encourage deep understanding of fundamental principles and their practical implications in humanoid robotics.
